# N-CORE Playbook

A strategy only earns a place here after it survives walk-forward
validation: the engine trains on the first 70% of an asset's history,
then gets tested cold on the held-out last 30% it never saw. To pass,
that out-of-sample test window needs at least 10 trades, a 55%+ win
rate, and a profit factor of 1.3 or better. Assets that don't clear
the bar are left alone — standing aside is the honest answer when
nothing validated.

**Criteria:** 70/30 split · min 10 test trades · min 55% win rate · min 1.3 profit factor
